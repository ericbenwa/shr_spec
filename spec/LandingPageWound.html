Skin and Wound CIMI-FHIR Implementation Guide (IG)

Patient Care Work Group and the Clinical Information Modeling Initiative (CIMI) are conducting a demonstration project involving Skin and Wound (SW) Assessment clinical domain. The purpose of this project is to compare different ways to model and develop FHIR profiles for the SW domain. Several groups, using different modeling tools, are participating in this demonstation. The goal is to learn what tools and modeling styles are most effective.

Background
